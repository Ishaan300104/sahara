💡 Coping Tips

🌬️ Box Breathing: Inhale 4s → Hold 4s → Exhale 4s → Hold 4s. Repeat 4 times to instantly reduce anxiety.
📵 Digital Detox: Put your phone face-down for 30 minutes before bed. Blue light disrupts melatonin and sleep quality.
🚶 5-Minute Walk: Even a short walk outside can reduce cortisol levels and reset your focus.
✍️ Brain Dump: Write down everything on your mind for 5 minutes. Externalizing worry reduces mental load.
🍵 Pomodoro + Break: Study 25 minutes, rest 5. Sustainable focus beats marathon cramming sessions.
